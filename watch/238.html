The Godfather

Action Sci-Fi Thriller 2010 8.8/10

The Godfather Is A Gripping Crime Drama That Chronicles The Rise And Fall Of The Corleone Family, One Of The Most Powerful Mafia Dynasties In America. When An Attempt On Don Vito Corleone’s Life Shakes The Family, His Youngest Son Michael—Once Determined To Live Outside The World Of Organized Crime—Is Drawn Reluctantly Into The Business. As He Seeks Revenge And Protects His Family, Michael’s Transformation Into A Ruthless And Calculated Leader Forever Changes The Legacy Of The Corleones. A Masterpiece Of Power, Loyalty, And Betrayal, The Film Explores How Family Bonds Can Become Entwined With Violence And Corruption.
